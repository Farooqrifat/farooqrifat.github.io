UWL Fitness App

For my final project at University i decided to develop the UWL Fitness application to drive more University of West London students into fitness and a healthier lifestyle. I believe that every establishment must have a mobile application for people to use available to them as everything is becoming digitalized. As a Computer Science student that worked at the UWL Fitness Centre, I believe that developing this application will help people get into a healthier lifestyle as well as solve many problems we faced in the fitness centre.
